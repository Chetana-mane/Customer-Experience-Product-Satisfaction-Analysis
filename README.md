# Customer Experience & Product Satisfaction Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer satisfaction, renewal experience, and product perception using Net Promoter Score (NPS) analytics for a healthcare insurance service provider.

The analysis was conducted using Python, Power BI, and VBA to identify the key drivers of customer loyalty, dissatisfaction, and retention. The project focuses on uncovering customer pain points related to pricing, renewal communication, customer support responsiveness, and loyalty recognition.

The solution transforms raw customer survey data into actionable business insights through segmentation analysis, sentiment evaluation, and interactive dashboards.

---

## Business Problem

Healthcare insurance providers often struggle to maintain customer satisfaction due to:
- Rising premium costs
- Poor renewal communication
- Lack of perceived value among non-claim customers
- Long customer support wait times
- Weak loyalty recognition for long-term customers

The objective of this project was to:
- Identify the main drivers of customer satisfaction and dissatisfaction
- Analyze customer behavior and renewal patterns
- Detect high-risk customer segments
- Build interactive dashboards for stakeholder reporting
- Recommend product and customer experience improvements

## Methodology

### 1. Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Removed incomplete records
- Standardized categorical fields
- Created customer segmentation groups
- Prepared datasets for dashboard reporting

### 2. NPS Analysis
Customers were categorized into:
- Promoters (9–10)
- Passives (7–8)
- Detractors (0–6)

NPS Formula:

NPS = % Promoters - % Detractors

### 3. Customer Segmentation
Customers were segmented based on:
- Claim status
- Renewal method
- Customer tenure
- Satisfaction scores

### 4. Sentiment Analysis
Performed text analysis on customer feedback to identify:
- Common complaints
- Satisfaction drivers
- Pricing concerns
- Renewal experience issues

---

## Key Insights

### Overall NPS Score
- Total Responses: 231
- Promoters: 45%
- Passives: 26%
- Detractors: 30%
- Overall NPS: +15

### Major Dissatisfaction Drivers
- Rising premiums
- Poor pricing transparency
- Long support wait times
- Lack of loyalty rewards

### Customer Segmentation Findings
- Customers who claimed insurance services had significantly higher satisfaction
- Long-tenure customers showed lower satisfaction due to premium increases
- Customers unaware of renewal processes had the lowest ratings

### Renewal Experience Findings
- Auto-renew customers reported higher satisfaction
- Customers confused about renewals were more likely to become detractors
